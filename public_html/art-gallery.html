Take this as my invitation for you to spend some time exploring some of the creations I’ve made over the years, some of which I’m just too proud of to not share. GOLD STAR FOR EFFORT

Medium: Digital picture, altered with iPhone, 4/2023

Artist: Stephanie Miranda

From the industrial art district, RiNo in Denver, CO, this sign spoke to me on a bright an sun shining day. The building’s location and weeds that surround the entrance were in stark contrast to the warm breaking up the winter blues. Rather than an actual sticker, I captured this image to bring joy.
